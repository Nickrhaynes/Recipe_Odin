Maple Granola

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 2/3 cup chopped nuts od choice

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 315 degrees F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. In a large bowl mix together canola oil, maple syrup, cinnamon and salt
  3. Add rolled oats and nuts and mix until combined
  4. Spread out mixture on baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through
  5. Allow to cool for 20 minutes.
