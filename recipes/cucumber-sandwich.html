Cucumber Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 Tablespoon plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 Tablespoon sliced fresh chives
  • 1 Tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1/3 cup thinly sliced English cucumber

Directions

  1. Stir cream cheese, yogurt, chives, dill and pepper together in a small bowl until well blended
  2. Spread mixture evenly on one side of each bread slice
  3. Top one slice with cucumber slices, then top with the other slice, cream cheese-side down
